Engineering software for teams building autonomous machines

Explore gets you the right spec. Complete builds the whole machine.

Sture Complete is the deterministic engine and review loop that runs the rest of the lifecycle: architecture, detailed design, simulation, sourcing, firmware, test, compliance, deployment, and the field-feedback loop back into the next revision. Every step traceable, every result reproducible.

8 November is the birthday of Sture, the grandfather this company is named for.

Everything in Explore, plus the engine that builds the machine.

Explore takes you from a problem to an approved, evidence-backed specification. Complete adds the deterministic Engine Layer and the AI-Layer-2 advisor loop, and runs the rest of the lifecycle, from architecture through telemetry, with the reasoning still attached to every decision.

Architecture and design

  • Architecture trade study (vehicle class, power, propulsion, sensing, compute, comms, safety).
  • Detailed design: schematic, PCB, harness, BOM.
  • Multi-board split detection (the 14-pattern daughter-board table).
  • Mechanical: frame, chassis, hull, airframe, enclosures, mounting, CAD sync (STEP/glTF, SOLIDWORKS/PDM read, bidirectional write).
  • Propulsion and actuation sizing, sensor placement and field-of-view reasoning.

Validation and sourcing

  • Simulation and analysis: power, endurance, thermal, dynamics and control, RF and comms, structural and vibration, environmental.
  • Sourcing: purchasable parts, alternates, lead-time, cost and availability, local supply chain, manufacturing exports.

Software, test, compliance, deploy

  • Firmware and autonomy-stack generation (ROS 2, PX4, Nav2, MAVLink, RTOS and embedded Linux), calibration and bring-up.
  • Test planning: bench, subsystem, integration, safety, and field tests, acceptance criteria, operator checklists.
  • Compliance evidence: standards mapping, safety case, FMEA and hazard analysis, ICD, operating envelope.
  • Deployment package: setup, calibration, preflight and predrive checks, operator procedures, maintenance, rollback.

Closed loop and platform

  • Telemetry ingestion (SITL, HIL, field) and predicted-vs-observed comparison.
  • Redesign recommendations feeding governed rule improvement.
  • Reproducible, signed generation trace (spec, engine, rule, corpus and component versions, artifacts, evidence bundle).
  • Canonical ReadinessProofReport as the final readiness authority.
  • Adapter and external-compute layer for long-running simulators and GPU jobs.
  • Deep air, ground, and water domain packs on the shared core.
